Nissan Thrilling Designs Campaign | 2023

Client: Nissan USA

Agency: Fluent360

Role: Social Media Marketing Strategist

In 2023, Nissan set out to highlight how they break the mold in car design, with a focus on connecting authentically with diverse audiences. As part of this effort, we crafted a dynamic, culturally resonant campaign that leveraged key influencers and brand ambassadors to tell Nissan’s story of innovation and creativity.

Targeting the Hispanic Audience and Engaging the Black Community:

We spotlighted Alfonso Albaisa, Nissan’s VP of Global Design and a prominent Latino figure in the automotive world. Through Alfonso's voice, we showcased the craftsmanship and passion that go into Nissan's designs, creating a deeper connection with Hispanic audiences by highlighting his cultural influence and personal journey.

Partnering with actor Jay Ellis, we amplified Nissan’s message of breaking every mold, focusing on the creativity and forward-thinking designs of their vehicles. Jay Ellis’ trusted and relatable persona helped forge a strong connection with the Black community, reinforcing Nissan’s commitment to innovative design that transcends expectations.

Digital Execution:

This campaign was created across social media platforms and integrated into Nissan’s broader digital strategy. The content was tailored for both English and Spanish-speaking audiences, ensuring cultural relevance and resonance across diverse markets.
